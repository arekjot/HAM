About Me

Hi there! My name is Arek, and my callsign is SP7AJ. I'm located in Łódź, Poland.

I'm passionate about almost everything in ham radio, but CW, DXing and QRP is something that I like the most. CW is my main working mode and HF are fav bands. However, you can also hear me on VHF/UHF, mostly ragchewing locally or testing my equipment.

Speaking of equipment, that’s another chapter - I love collecting vintage ham radio gear, especially handhelds from the '80s and '90s. My collection is yet small, but I hope it will only keep growing!

As the layout of this page may suggest, I'm also a fan of retro vibes in general - whether related to electronics or the internet.

I'm also a proud member of CWOps (#3496) and SP DX Club (#976).

Looking forward to hearing you on the bands and I hope you like my page!
